Packing Essentials

February in Orlando: 50s–70°F, possible rain. Layers are key. Full packing list available in the planning folder — here are the highlights.

Park Day Bag

  • Backpack (lightweight, comfy)
  • Refillable water bottles (free ice water at any QS!)
  • Ponchos for everyone (buy cheap before trip)
  • Portable phone chargers (2+ per family)
  • Sunscreen SPF 50+
  • Snacks (granola bars, goldfish, fruit snacks)
  • Wet wipes — essential with 5 kids
  • Autograph books + thick Sharpies
  • Glow sticks for evening magic

DVC Villa Essentials

DVC rental = limited housekeeping! Bring these:

  • Laundry detergent pods (3–4 loads/villa)
  • Extra trash bags (13 gal, fills fast!)
  • Paper towels (2+ rolls/villa)
  • Dish soap / dishwasher pods
  • Clorox wipes (counters, spills)
  • Extra toilet paper (2–3 rolls)
  • Hand soap refill
  • Ziplock bags (gallon + quart)

Don't Forget!

  • Break in ALL shoes 2+ weeks before trip
  • Charge MagicBands night before departure
  • Download offline content to kids' tablets
  • Buy ponchos, glow sticks, autograph books BEFORE the trip
  • Stroller for Clara and Mia (10+ miles/day!)
  • Check weather forecast 3 days before
